Servicios Clinicos

Spanish Clinic ofrece servicios de terapia que están basados en la teoria Cognitiva del Comportamiento, puesto que esta teoria ayuda a los clientes a re-estructurar sus patrones de pensamiento y por tanto sus comportamientos. Nustos programas estan enfocados en el desarrollo de habilidades para que los clientes las puedan desarrollar en las situacioens de su vida.

Spanish Clinic serves ofrece servicios en áreas en las que creemos que hay necesidad de servicios bilingues y bi-culturales. Nosotros creemos que uno de los aspectos esenciales en el proceso de cambio esta basad en entender su cultura y sus ambientes culturales. Esta institución quiere ofrecer educación y terapia sobre alcohol, drogas y salud mental en un ambiente culturalmente apropiado para los clientes. Nosotros ofrecemos programas en el área de adicciones y salud mental a la población en general, esecialmente a menores y mujeres.

Clinical Services

Spanish Clinic offers treatment that is Cognitive-Behavioral based; as this helps clients restructure their thought patterns and therefore their behavior. Our treatment is also focused on skills building, so the theory can be applied to each individual’s life situation.

Spanish Clinic serves in an area that is thought to be in need of bilingual and bicultural services. It is the belief of Spanish Clinic that one of the main venues for change derives from understanding an individual's culture and cultural environment. This agency wants to deliver education and treatment regarding drugs and alcohol in a culturally appropriate environment for all clients. Spanish Clinic offers programs in the areas of addictions and mental health to the general population and to women and minors.

Translations

Spanish Clinic will develop a bridge between two languages: English and Spanish. We are highly competitive on Translations from English to Spanish not only because of our native language or because of our strong educational background, but because we are bicultural. In order to provide an estimate we consider the following aspects:

Contents: General information not published. Published information. Technical information such as Legal or Medical.

Length: Less than 300 words. Between 300 to 1000 words More than 1000 words. For documents over 5000 words, please contact our office for an estimate. Note: The charge per word applies to the number of words in the document to be translated.

Design: Plain document Designed document.

Time: Regular or Expedited

The design is a factor difficult to evaluate and will be evaluated on case by case documents. The following table provides an estimate for documents without special design and in a regular time providing a NEW SPECIAL RATE:
